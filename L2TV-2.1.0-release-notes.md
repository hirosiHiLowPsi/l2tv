# L2TV 2.1.0

## IR and Rival

- Restored Stellaverse IR rank and IR status displays.
- Restored Stellaverse Rival ID comparison with public scores, grade, FORCE RATE, title, and badge.
- IR rank, IR status, and Stellaverse Rival currently support Stellaverse IR only.
- IR rank and IR status can be toggled independently from the menu.
- When both IR displays are disabled, L2TV skips fetching the player's IR rankings.

## FORCE RATE

- Added comparison with the previous load's FORCE RATE.
- Added IN / OUT lists for charts entering or leaving the FORCE RATE targets.
- FORCE RATE screenshots now include the current title badge, rating, previous-load change, and IN / OUT information.

## UI

- Reorganized the fixed controls into a scroll-aware application toolbar.
- Grouped menu settings into loading, difficulty tables, display, and other settings.
- Added compact table header metrics for chart count, clear rate, and played rate.
- Adjusted summary card spacing and text alignment.
- Unified level-folder corners and kept wide chart tables inside their folder boundaries.
- Reduced visual weight in regular chart rows while retaining clear lamp colors in FORCE RATE target rows.

## Distribution

- Version: `2.1.0`
- Windows portable archive: `L2TV-2.1.0-win-x64.7z`
- Format: 7z, no installer required.
